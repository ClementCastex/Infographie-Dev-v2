Activity Sectors by scale of Companies

Localization of Companies

Now that we've seen where students do their apprenticeships and the number who go each year, we may notice that the majority of companies are small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), accounting for 74%. This means that a large portion of companies hiring apprentices are in a good position to do so.

(Source: NWS Database & French Business Directory)

After reassuring students about the stability of the number of apprentices, it's noteworthy that most companies are located in Rouen and its Metropolitan area, with a limited presence of apprentices outside the 76 and 27 departments of Normandy. This might reassure some students who haven't obtained their driver's license.

(Source: NWS Database & French Business Directory)
